myHealthcare

Our goal is to provide clients with an application that would help them monitor their health and make health maintenance more organized and streamlined. Rather than having to scramble around collecting information and making a bunch of phone calls, they could go online and have everything that they need in one consolidated platform.

Features

Everything you need in one platform

Physician Search

Users are given the tools to search for healthcare providers in their area. This tool also comes with a rank and review section.

Prescription Tracker

The user is able to track their prescriptions and use the application to order more if necessary. The application also comes with a refill reminder system.

Appointment Manager

The user can keep track of all upcoming appointments on the calendar. They are also given recommendations on when they should schedule an appointment with another physician according to their last visit.

Health Tracker

Users have the option of connecting their smartwatch to the application so that they can monitor their health. Blood pressure, sleep history, and activity levels are the statuses being measured using their device.

Record Keeper

Health insurance and all of the user’s EMR’s are stored in one area. The user is also able to ensure that their insurance is compatible with the physician that they wish to visit.

Payment Manager

The application will allow clients to pay bills through the app and/or link them to the party requiring payment.
